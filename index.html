The Problem

You're Not Capturing It.

Visitors love your estate. They post, they share, they recommend to friends — and platforms capture all the upside. The tasting room closes at 5 pm. Direct-to-consumer is expensive. And affiliate programs feel cheap. AoW changes the equation.

02

Building an audience, running ads, managing influencers — it's a second business. Most wineries can't staff it, let alone make it profitable.

03

Commissions are a black box

Traditional affiliate programs offer opaque last-click logic, unpredictable payouts, and no way to understand who actually drove the sale.

Three-tier markups and crowded shelf space leave wineries dependent on distributors. Direct commerce is the future — but the path there isn't clear.

Common Questions

Answers for
Winery Teams.

Do we need to hold or ship inventory differently?+

No. AoW integrates with your existing fulfillment — Shopify, WooCommerce, or your current DTC setup. You handle fulfillment exactly as you do today. AoW handles the discovery and attribution layer.

Is this affiliate marketing?+

No. It's a marketplace with transparent, rule-based attribution and trackable commission payouts. You set your commission structure. The protocol enforces it. Agents see exactly what they earn and why.

Do we need compliance approval for commission-based wine sales?+

AoW is structured as a Coselling and content commerce platform — not a broker, distributor, or retailer. All transactions flow through your existing DTC infrastructure (Shopify, WooCommerce, etc.), and you retain full control over pricing, fulfillment, and compliance. The platform operates within standard direct-to-consumer frameworks used by wineries today. We recommend confirming with your compliance counsel, and our team is available to walk through the structure in detail during onboarding.

How much does it cost for a winery to join?+

Founding Circle wineries get priority access and locked-in merchant pricing. We'll share full pricing details during your onboarding call. We are aligned in how we make money: AoW earns when you earn.

What if we only have a small SKU count or a boutique operation?+

Boutique wineries are exactly who AoW is built for. You don't need volume — you need the right advocates. Even a focused portfolio of 8 to 12 wines can build a powerful presence in the Hive.

When does income start?+

We are onboarding founding merchants now and targeting income generation within 1–2 months of launch. Founding Circle wineries will be the first active in the marketplace.
